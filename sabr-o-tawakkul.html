Patience and Trust in Allah

Patience (sabr) and trust in Allah (tawakkul) are among the fundamental teachings of Islam. The Qur'an mentions in several places Allah's special companionship and reward for those who are patient.

The Reality of Patience

Patience does not mean running away from difficulties but enduring them while remaining content with Allah's decree. It is not weakness but a sign of strong faith.

The Meaning of Trust in Allah

Tawakkul means adopting the necessary means while leaving the outcome entirely to Allah. In other words, effort is the human's responsibility, but the result lies in Allah's hands.

Application in Daily Life

Adopting patience and trust in Allah during difficult circumstances brings peace to the heart and helps one avoid unnecessary worry. These are the very qualities that keep a person steadfast through every trial.
