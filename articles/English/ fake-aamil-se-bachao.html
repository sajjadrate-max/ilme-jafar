Protecting Yourself from Fraudulent Practitioners

It is unfortunate that society sees many cases of fraud and deception in the name of spiritual treatment and practitioners (aamils). Knowing the signs of such fraudulent practitioners and how to guard against them is essential.

Common Signs of a Fraudulent Practitioner

Demanding large sums of money, insisting on secrecy, having people perform acts that contradict Islamic law, and guaranteeing a quick and certain result are generally regarded as signs of fake and fraudulent practitioners.

Ways to Stay Safe

For any spiritual matter, consult only those who adhere to basic religious teachings, do not have you perform anything against Sharia, and are transparent in financial dealings.

Important Advice

The safest and most authentic approach is for a person to recite the Qur'an, recommended supplications, and remembrances themselves, and for any serious matter, to consult directly with a reputable religious institution or a trustworthy scholar.
