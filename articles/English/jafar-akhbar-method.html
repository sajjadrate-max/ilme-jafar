The Method of Ilm-e-Jafar Akhbar

Ilm-e-Jafar Akhbar is one of the two main branches of traditional Ilm-e-Jafar. In this method, the letters of the question asked are arranged in a particular sequence.

The Method

First, the question is written out in Arabic or Urdu script. The letters are then arranged into a staircase-style grid. From this grid, every fourth letter is extracted, and these extracted letters together form a new combination.

How the Result Is Interpreted

The resulting set of letters is analyzed according to traditional rules. Each letter carries a traditional spiritual nature and tendency (positive, negative, or neutral), and the overall tendency of all these letters together is used to derive an answer to the question.

A Word of Caution

This is a traditional technique that should be approached with seriousness and sincerity. Its result should be treated as guidance rather than a final verdict, and important decisions should always also include prayer, istikhara, and consultation with people of knowledge.
